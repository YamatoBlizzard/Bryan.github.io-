God of War

What is it?

After the events in Greece Kratos, the god of war, was found wondering around Midgard. He later on met a female warrior named Faye. After witnessing her in battle and how she fights, he fell in love with her. They then started to live in the forest away from unwanting eyes. Later on they had a son. The boy body was weak and easily get sick so he was not able to leave the hut. When Faye taught her son how hunt, she mysteriously died. With Faye dead her son how will he build up his relationship with his father. How will Kratos raise his son, when he has a dark past.

Gameplay

The gameplay is flawless and easy to pick up. There are different ways to attack your enemies and executing them. When you progress further into the game there will be additional abilities and power ups to combo to make create more devastating combos.

Summary

God of War is different from the previous games through out the years and changed a lot. With the game now focusing on the story a lot more about the relationship and gameplay is fun for new and old God of War fans.
