Spiderman

About

It is a single player open world game. It tells the story of Spiderman and the life of Peter Parker all together. It takes places a few years after Peter was a highschooler and now a university student. With that Spiderman has alot more fighting style and alot more gadgets to neutralize his enemies. When playing Spiderman it feels good swinging from building to building, as there are different tick you can do to earn xp points.

Gameplay

How you play Spiderman is up to you. If you wanna aggressive or stealth the games offers a variety of gadgets to make how you play Spiderman. There is a mixed of offense and defense as some enemies require you to be caution.
