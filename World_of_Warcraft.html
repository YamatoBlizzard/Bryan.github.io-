World of Warcraft

What is it?

World of Warcraft is an MMORPG(Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game). In the game their world is know as Azeroth, where the world of men, elves, gnomes, and dwarfs live in peace until one day the orcs came through the dark portal. The world of Azeroth is no longer the same as war is coming, old legends that should have kept hidden, and the future of Azeroth.

Players can choose from two different faction. The Alliance or the Horde.
Each faction have different races and each race has a personal racials different from other races.

The factions

The Alliance

The Alliance values peace, equality and order. They are friendly, kind, and strong when fighting together. They will fight for what they believe is right and help those who can't defend themselves.

The Horde

The Horde values strengh and honor. They are cunning and ruthless in battle. They were treated differently from the Alliance and band together to protect their new home.
